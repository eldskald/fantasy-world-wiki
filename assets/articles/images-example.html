Images Example

This article is made to showcase putting images in your article. You can see how images look like here in this page and you can see how to put them in your article on the source file at assets/articles/images-example.html. Keep in mind floating images become full size images on mobile.

Floating images

You make floating images by adding the right-float or left-float class to the image. It only affects images on PC, on mobile all images are full size. A floating image will try to keep it's original size unless it's width is bigger than 512px, that's half the width of the article popup window. If that's the case, they will be maxed out to 512px.
